No Body, No Crime

written by: Taylor Swift
Album: Evermore

Channeling noir storytelling, "No Body, No Crime" is an upbeat country, country rock, and pop rock murder ballad that details the fictitious story of a woman named Este, who is killed by her own husband when she finds out about his infidelity.
